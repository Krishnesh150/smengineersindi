Level Gauge


It is used measure and display the level of a liquid or bulk solid material in a container or vessel. Level gauges are essential in various industrial and commercial applications to monitor and control fluid levels for safety, process control, and inventory management. Here are some key points about level gauges

Types of Level Gauges:

  • Sight Glass
  • Float and Tape
  • Magnetic Level Gauge
  • Ultrasonic Level Sensor
  • Radar Level Sensor
  • Capacitance Level Sensor
