Temprature Gauge


A temperature gauge is a device or instrument used to measure and display the temperature of a particular environment or substance. It's a common tool used in various industries, scientific research, and everyday applications to monitor temperature levels accurately. Temperature gauges are essential in situations where maintaining specific temperature ranges is crucial for safety, efficiency, or quality control.

There are several types of temperature gauges, each using different principles to measure temperature:

  • Bimetallic Strip Thermometer
  • Thermocouples
  • Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs)
  • Infrared Thermometers
