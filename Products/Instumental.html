Pressure Gauge

Pressure gauges are instruments used to measure and display the pressure of a medium. Pressure gauges are pressure measuring instruments with elastic pressure elements, which have been used millions of times in various industrial applications.

Tempreture Gauge

A temperature gauge is a device used to measure and read temperature gradient accurately. It usually refers to a device that shows readings on a numbered dial, and it is commonly found in industrial and commercial settings

Level Gauge

A level gauge is a sensing and measuring instrument used to detect the level of a fluid or gas in a tank or a storage container. These devices are widely used in industrial process applications.

Flow Meter

A flow meter is a device used to measure the flow rate of fluids in pipes . It provides information about the volume, mass, or velocity of the fluid being measured. Flow meters are essential in industries such as manufacturing, gas &power.

RTD

RTD often stands for "Resistance Temperature Detector." It is a type of temperature sensor used to measure temperature by correlating the resistance of a platinum or other metal wire with temperature.

Pressure Transmitter

A pressure transmitter, is a device used to measure and convert the pressure of a fluid into an electrical signal. It is commonly employed in industrial and process control applications to monitor and control pressure in various systems.

Pressure Switch

A pressure switch is a type of control device that is commonly used in various industrial, commercial, and residential applications to monitor and control the pressure of a fluid (usually a gas or liquid) within a system.

Instument Fittings

Instrument fittings are components used in industrial and process instrumentation systems to connect, secure, and ensure the proper functioning of various instruments and devices.

Instument Valves

Instrument valves are essential components in industrial and process instrumentation systems that control the flow, pressure, and direction of fluids and gases.

Level Switch

A level switch is a type of control device used to detect and monitor the level of liquids, solids, or slurries in containers, tanks, or vessels. It helps maintain proper levels, prevent overflow or empty conditions, and can trigger various actions or alarms based on the detected level.

Venturi Tube

A Venturi tube, also known as a Venturi meter, is a flow measurement device used to determine the flow rate of a fluid (liquid or gas) passing through a pipe or conduit.

Thermoweell

A thermowell is a protective device used in industrial and process applications to shield temperature sensors, such as thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors (RTDs), from the harsh or corrosive environments of the process fluid.

Ultrasonic Level Transmitter

An ultrasonic level transmitter is a type of non-contact level measurement device that uses ultrasonic sound waves to determine the distance between the transmitter and the surface of a liquid or solid material.

Ultrasonic Flow Transmitter

An ultrasonic flow transmitter is a type of flow measurement device that uses ultrasonic sound waves to measure the velocity of a fluid flowing within a pipe or conduit.

Solonoid Valves

Solenoid valves are electromechanical devices that are commonly used to control the flow of liquids or gases. They work by using an electromagnetic coil to generate a magnetic field, which in turn moves a plunger or a piston within the valve.

Indiactor

An indicator is a device that provides visual or digital information about a specific parameter, state, or condition. A totalizer is a device used to keep track of the cumulative total of a specific parameter over time.

Hydrostatic Level Transmitter

An ultrasonic level transmitter is a type of non-contact level measurement device that uses ultrasonic sound waves to determine the distance between the transmitter and the surface of a liquid or solid material.

Radar Level Transmitter

A radar level transmitter is an advanced sensor used for non-contact level measurement of liquids, solids, or slurries in various industrial applications. It employs radar waves to determine the distance between the sensor and the surface of the material being measured.

Level Transmitter

"Level transmitter" is a broad term that encompasses various devices used to measure and transmit information about the level of a substance in a container or vessel.

Instrument cables

Instrument cables, often referred to as "instrument patch cables" or "instrument cords," are specialized cables used to connect musical instruments to amplifiers, effects pedals, audio interfaces, and other audio equipment.

Thermocouple

A thermocouple is a type of temperature sensor that consists of two different metal wires joined at one end. When there is a temperature difference between the two ends of the wires, it generates a voltage that is proportional to the temperature difference. This voltage can then be measured and used to determine the temperature at the junction of the two wires.

Totaliser

A totalizer is a device used to accumulate and display a running total or sum of quantities or events over time. While a totalizer can serve as an indicator, its primary function is to provide cumulative information. Totalizers are commonly used in various applications, including counting, volume measurement, distance measurement, time measurement, and energy usage tracking, as mentioned earlier.
